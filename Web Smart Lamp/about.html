Introduction

The use of LED lights in classrooms serves a functional purpose but also presents its own challenges. From an electrical consumption perspective, if LED lights are used excessively, it can result in significant electricity usage. The Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources notes that even a 10% electricity saving can lead to a budget saving of up to IDR 43.000.000.000.000,00 (Forty-Three Trillion Rupiah) annually. This is equivalent to the electricity produced by a power plant with a capacity of 3,500 megawatts (MW). There are three ways to save electricity consumption: First, turning off lights when not in use; second, setting the AC temperature to 25 degrees Celsius; and third, using electronic devices efficiently.

Observing the positive impact derived from simple actions, such as turning off lights when not in use, our group decided to apply this principle to the smart lamp system. This smart lamp system incorporates several sensors, including those designed to detect the presence of individuals and to monitor ambient light levels. The system will be integrated with a real-time clock module, enabling scheduling. All the sensors and modules will be integrated into a website, allowing remote control of the smart lamp.

By incorporating these sensors, the expected outcome for the lamp that will be created is expected to become more efficient and energy-saving. For example, when there is no one in a room, the lamp will remain off, resulting in electricity savings. Additionally, the lamp will automatically turn on when the ambient light conditions are dim. Scheduling can be employed to regulate the specific times when the lamp should be turned on or off, such as turning off at 7 a.m. and turning on again at 6 p.m. These features can be accessed by the user through a website, thereby optimizing the control functionality of this smart lamp system.

Tujuan Project Smart LampU

Kami bertujuan untuk membuat sebuah sistem otomasi lampu. Dimana dengan sistem yang kami kembangkan ini, dapat mengefisienkan penggunaan dari listrik yang digunakan untuk menyalakan Lampu . Selain itu sistem ini juga dibuat untuk memudahkan untuk mengakses pencahayaan. Dengan sistem ini dapat dikontrol secara otomatis menggunakan sensor-sensor juga dapat diakses secara mobile dari website.
